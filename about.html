About the Founder

James Harrison, MBA

James Harrison is the Founder and CEO of Nabzit. With extensive experience in executive leadership, operational excellence, and global management, James brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the company. His career includes significant achievements in various industries, demonstrating his ability to drive transformation and growth.

James attended the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst, where he studied Strategic Leadership and Management before commissioning into the British Army. He has since held executive roles across diverse sectors, including logistics, IT, manufacturing, and financial services, among others.

At Nabzit, James is focused on developing a platform that enhances consumer convenience and meets the evolving needs of online shoppers through AI-driven solutions.

James was recognised as the Outstanding Achiever of the Year at the Ex-Forces in Business Awards in 2020 and was a finalist for the Institute of Directors - Director of the Year in 2019.
