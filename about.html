About Nabzit

James Harrison, Founder & CEO of Nabzit

James Harrison is a visionary leader with a passion for leveraging technology to drive meaningful change. With a diverse background in logistics, IT, manufacturing, construction, transport, financial services, media & telecommunications, utilities, defence, and software, James brings a wealth of experience to Nabzit. As the founder and CEO, he is dedicated to revolutionising the online shopping experience through innovative AI solutions.

James holds an MBA and has a proven track record of leading transformative initiatives across various industries. His strategic mindset and operational expertise have been instrumental in driving growth and optimising business processes.

In recognition of his exceptional contributions, James was honoured with the Ex-Forces in Business Outstanding Achiever of the Year award in 2020 and was a finalist for the Institute of Directors - Director of the Year award in 2019.

James attended the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst where he studied Strategic Leadership and Management before commissioning into the British Army. His global experience, both during his military service and in his subsequent executive roles, has equipped him with a unique perspective and leadership style.

At Nabzit, James is focused on developing a platform that provides a superior shopping experience, meeting the evolving needs of consumers. Under his leadership, Nabzit is poised to become a game-changer in the world of online shopping.

Vision for Nabzit

At Nabzit, our mission is to revolutionise the online shopping experience by leveraging cutting-edge AI technology to provide consumers with the best deals effortlessly. We are committed to creating a user-centric platform that simplifies the shopping process and supports the evolving needs of today's consumers.
